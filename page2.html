Who is Thor Odinson?

About Thor

Thor Odinson is the God of Thunder and storms from norse mythology. Hes the son of Odin and the heir to Asgard.

Thor's strengths

Thor is the strongest norse god and possesses the ability to control lightning and storms. He is also a skilled warrior and a protector of both Asgard and Earth.

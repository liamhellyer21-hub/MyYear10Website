Who is Thor Odinson?

About Thor

Thor is the god of thunder and storms in Norse mythology. He is the son of Odin and is known as one of the strongest gods. Thor protects both Asgard and Midgard from giants and other threats.

Thor's strengths

Thor is one of the strongest gods in Norse mythology and can control lightning and storms with his power. He is also a skilled warrior who protects both Asgard and Midgard from giants and other enemies.

Thor's weaknesses

Thor is sometimes prone to anger and impulsiveness, and he can be foolish, which allows his enemies to outsmart him. He is also vulnerable to powerful magic and strong poisons, such as the venom of the serpent Jörmungandr.
